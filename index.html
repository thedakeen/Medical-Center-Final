Welcome to MEDICODE

We strive to help our patients improve their standard of living by providing quality medical services, combining them with an atmosphere of comfort, a staff of highly qualified specialists, expert-class equipment and modern diagnostic and treatment methods from 08.00 to 20.00, 6 days a week.

By contacting the specialists of our Medical Center, patients receive not only high-quality medical services, including a comprehensive examination and accurate diagnosis, but also comprehensive treatment using the latest methods with further recommendations for disease prevention.

Equipping the clinic with modern equipment allows you to quickly and clearly make a diagnosis, conduct constant monitoring and provide high-quality treatment. Diagnostic examination and treatment are selected strictly individually for each person, taking into account many indicators: gender, age, health status, and so on. All departments of the medical center work in close connection with each other, which allows you to quickly resolve issues that arise during the treatment process.

A high level of safety, at all stages of treatment carried out with minimal risk of complications and an accelerated rehabilitation process, the treatment process is carried out in the Medical Center with maximum efficiency.
